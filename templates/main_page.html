About us

The Russian Constructicon is a free and publicly available resource for learners of Russian as well as for instructors and linguists. The Russian Constructicon contains over 1000 grammatical constructions (multiword expressions that are not transparent to L2 learners, such as возьми и VP in он возьми и купи себе новую машину ‘geez, he went and bought himself a new car’) with explanations and illustrative examples. The database is being constantly updated with the new constructions added to the website.

Old version of the Russian Constructicon is available here.

The Russian Constructicon is a joint project of Higher School of Economics (Moscow) and UiT The Arctic University of Norway (Tromsø).
